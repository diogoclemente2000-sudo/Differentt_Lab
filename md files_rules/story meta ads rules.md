# 📱 Meta Ads — Story Format Guidelines (Instagram & Facebook)

## 🎯 Objective

Create high-converting Story ads optimized for visibility, readability, and user interaction.

Focus on:

* Clear messaging
* Strong visuals
* Correct safe zone usage
* Mobile-first design

---

## 📐 Format Specifications

* Aspect Ratio: **9:16 (Vertical)**
* Resolution: **1080 x 1920 px**
* Format: JPG / PNG / MP4
* Duration (video): up to 15 seconds per story

---

## ⚠️ Safe Zones (VERY IMPORTANT)

Avoid placing important content in these areas:

### 🔝 Top Area (UI Overlay Zone)

* Approx. top **250 px**
* Covered by:

  * Profile name
  * “Sponsored” label
  * Close button

👉 DO NOT place:

* Logos
* Headlines
* CTA

---

### 🔻 Bottom Area (CTA Overlay Zone)

* Approx. bottom **250 px**
* Covered by:

  * “Send Message”
  * “Learn More”
  * Swipe UI

👉 DO NOT place:

* Buttons
* Important text
* Offers

---

### ✅ Safe Content Area

* Center area (middle ~1400 px)
* This is where ALL important content must go

👉 Place here:

* Headlines
* Key message
* Product/service visuals
* CTA text

---

## 🎨 Design Guidelines

### Layout

* Keep content centered
* Use vertical stacking (top → middle → bottom flow)
* Avoid clutter

---

### Typography

* Large, bold text
* Max 2 fonts
* High contrast with background

---

### Colors

* Strong contrast (text vs background)
* Use brand colors
* Highlight CTA with accent color

---

## 🧠 Content Structure (Recommended)

### 1. Hook (Top-center)

* Short, impactful message
* Example:
  "Fix your phone in 15 minutes"
  "Find your dream home today"

---

### 2. Value Proposition (Center)

* What you offer
* Key benefit

---

### 3. CTA (Lower-center, above safe zone)

* "Book Now"
* "Send Message"
* "Get a Quote"

---

## ⚡ Motion (for video stories)

* Use subtle animations
* Fast-paced (grab attention in 1–2 seconds)
* Avoid slow intros

---

## 🔥 Conversion Tips

* Keep text minimal
* Focus on ONE message
* Use urgency:

  * "Limited offer"
  * "Today only"
* Add social proof if possible

---

## ❌ Avoid

* Text too close to edges
* Too much information
* Low contrast text
* Overcrowded layout
* Ignoring safe zones

---

## 🧪 Testing

Create multiple variations:

* Different headlines
* Different visuals
* Different CTAs

Test and optimize performance.

---

## 🎯 Final Goal

User sees → understands instantly → clicks CTA

Design must communicate in less than 3 seconds.
